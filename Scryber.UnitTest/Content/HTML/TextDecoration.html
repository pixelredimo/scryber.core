The following paragraphs have text decorations applied in areas by specific components

The underlines are performed by: The u(nderline) element, and also the ins(ert) element

The strike through is performed by: The s(trike) element, and also the del(ete) element

The highlight is performed by: The mark(ing) element that can flow across multiple lines in the document, and possibly even more lines within the page so that we can check this works. After the inline mark going onto further lines in the page.
